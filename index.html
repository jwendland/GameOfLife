The Game of Life

This is the Game of Life as invented by John Conway in 1970.

The Game of Life follows three simple rules:

  • A cell becomes alive if it has exactly three living neighbors.
  • A living cell will survive if it has two or three living neighbors.
  • A living cell will die in any other case due to missing resources or too many neighbors.
